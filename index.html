Boeing 747 Details

The Boeing 747 is a wide-body airliner designed and manufactured by Boeing Commercial Airplanes. It is known for its large capacity and is often used for long-haul flights.

  • Capacity: 400-500 passengers
  • Range: 8,000 miles
  • Engines: Four engines (various types)
Gulfstream G700 Details

The Gulfstream G700 is a luxury business jet with state-of-the-art technology and comfort. It provides a long-range travel option with spacious interiors.

  • Capacity: Up to 19 passengers
  • Range: 7,500 miles
  • Engines: Two Rolls-Royce Pearl 700 engines
Cessna Details

The Cessna 172 is a single-engine, high-wing aircraft that is widely used for flight training and personal use. Known for its reliability and ease of handling.

  • Capacity: 4 passengers
  • Range: 800 miles
  • Engine: One Lycoming IO-360
