About Us

AvBuyer.com offers the latest aircraft for sale from across the world. Plus the latest products and services for all private aircraft types from single piston to large jets.

Established for over 25 years, our byline is actionable intelligence which we deliver for aircraft buyers, sellers, owners and operators through print and digital magazines - AvBuyer and GABuyer Europe, live data feeds, online articles, videos and podcasts, buyers and price guides, specialist hubs and e-marketing.

AvBuyer.com offers the latest aircraft for sale from across the world. Plus the latest products and services for all private aircraft types from single piston to large jets.

Established for over 25 years, our byline is actionable intelligence which we deliver for aircraft buyers, sellers, owners and operators through print and digital magazines - AvBuyer and GABuyer Europe, live data feeds, online articles, videos and podcasts, buyers and price guides, specialist hubs and e-marketing.

Our editors and authors are the industry's leading aviation experts. And many have been writing for us for over a decade. Their insights are widely respected and rooted in their experience of working in the complex world of business aviation purchase, ownership, management and sales. Their advice has proved invaluable for buyers and sellers everywhere.

Our Editors:

Mike Chase, Editor, Aircraft Comparisons.
With over 30 years' experience Mike is the industry leader in aircraft comparative analysis for Jets, Turboprops and Helicopters.

David Wyndham, Editor, Ownership & Operating Costs.
David specializes in aircraft cost & performance analyses, fleet planning and lifecycle costing.

Brian Wilson, Editor, Jet Connectivity.
A senior executive at Gogo Business Aviation with over 40 years avionics experience, Brian is a font of knowledge on all things cabin connectivity.

Brian Foley, Editor, Market Intelligence.
A renowned aviation market analyst, Brian's views are shared and published in the media worldwide, including Forbes.

René Armas Maes, Editor, Buyer Strategy & Finance.
René develops and delivers commercial and consulting Business Aviation strategies for Fortune 500 companies, Venture Capital firms, and HNWIs.

Tony Kioussis, Editor, Aircraft Value & Maintenance Analysis.
Tony provides valuations, audits, analytics and consulting services, and a uniform methodology for grading an aircraft's maintenance condition.


Our Authors:

We have outstanding writers including Andre Fodor, David Higdon, Chris Kjelgaard, Fabrizio Poli, Gerrard Cowan, Jeremy Cox, Jet Tolbert, Ken Elliott, Mario Pierobon, AvBuyer Editor - Matt Harris, Patrick Ryan, all with extensive experience of writing on their respective fields of coverage.


Price and Buyers Guides, Specialist Hubs, Videos and Podcasts:

We offer a complete suite of tools to help prospective buyers, operators, owners and sellers:

Price & Market Data - Our aircraft listings pages feature live price and market data from the world's leading aviation data providers - JETNET, Aircraft Bluebook and Asset Insight - which provides buyers with the most relevant market data prior to submitting purchase enquiries.

Jet Buyer and Price Guides - The No 1 guides to the top jet models including live data.

Specialist Hubs and Digital editions - Our MRO, Engine Maintenance, Multi-Mission Aircraft, Finance and Avionics content provide in-depth analysis, FAQs and How-To guides in their respective areas. These are designed to offer a wealth of actionable information for those wanting to make smart ownership and operating choices with their aircraft.

Insiders' Guide Videos - Leading aviation broker, René Banglesdorf interviews the world's best private aircraft pilots who review their experiences flying the world's top business jets and turboprops.

Podcasts - We round up the best aviation podcasts online, including the latest episodes from Asset Insight.

Keep up-to-date with all the latest actionable intelligence from AvBuyer, sign up for our regular aircraft and editorial alerts.
